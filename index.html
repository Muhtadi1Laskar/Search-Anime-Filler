What is a Filler Episode

A filler episode is like a side story or bonus episode created for the TV show that isn't part of the main story in the book. It might be a fun adventure for the characters, a funny side story, or something else entirely.

In many cases, the production of the anime series outpaces the manga. Anime studios produce filler episodes in order to buy time until the manga series catches up.

The key points:

  • Regular episode: Follows the main plot of the story (like the book).
  • Filler episode: A separate story created for the show, not based on the main story.
